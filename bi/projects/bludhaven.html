Bludhaven State Penitentiary

By law in the United States, incarcerated individuals are entitled to healthcare services. The standards of care are set by state and federal law; county sheriffs offices or state DOCs face legal liability for not providing adequate medical or mental health care, and in many cases are also required to provide specialty rehabilitation and re-entry services to incarcerated individuals. It is common to see correctional facilities contract with third-party healthcare providers to deliver these services.

The vigilante known as Nightwing (formerly Robin, known associate of the Batman) regularly rounds up dangerous, super-powered criminals, leaving them in critical condition for BSP's corrections officers to deal with. In response, the penitentiary has contracted with a private specialty medical and mental health care provider - a subsidiary of LexCorp. The terms of the contract include requirements on the transparency of medical records data for every patient, and reports are to be made available to auditing bodies, correctional administrators, corporate leadership, and healthcare providers in order to ensure compliance with the contract and laws governing the standard of care.
