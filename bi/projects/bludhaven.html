Bludhaven State Penitentiary

The vigilante known as Nightwing (formerly Robin, known associate of the Batman) regularly rounds up dangerous, super-powered criminals, leaving them in critical condition for the city's officers to deal with. In response, the penitentiary has contracted with a private specialty medical and mental health care provider - a subsidiary of LexCorp. The terms of the contract include requirements on the transparency of medical records data for every patient, and reports are to be made available to auditing bodies, correctional administrators, corporate leadership, and healthcare providers in order to ensure compliance with the contract and laws governing the standard of care and the inmates' rights (super-powered or not).

LexCorp's electronic medical records (EMR) software is built upon a cloud-hosted postgres relational database with the following star schema:

Healthcare providers enter patient admissions, medical encounters, diagnoses, treatments, and outcomes into the EMR. Access to compliance reporting is enabled with a tech stack that includes spreadsheet software, business intelligence tools, automated emails, and the EMR itself. At all points of access, patient confidentiality and privacy are maintained in accordance with HIPAA regulations; encryption standards and row-level security are implemented to restrict access to only a minimum of authorized parties.
