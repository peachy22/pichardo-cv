Leading Development and GTM of a Healthcare Analytics Platform

BACKGROUND

NaphCare, LLC is a healthcare services provider and developer of the electronic medical record platform TechCare used in correctional facilities. Between 2023 and 2024 I led the conceptualization, development, and go-to-market strategy for TechCareBI, an analytics platform built on cloud frameworks and embedded directly within the TechCare ecosystem. The goal was to introduce operational analytics and predictive insights into the daily workflow of clinicians, administrators, and executive leadership.

Correctional healthcare environments involve complex intake processes, medication workflows, regulatory oversight, and strict documentation requirements. Historically, reporting in these environments was fragmented, retrospective, and often required manual compilation. TechCareBI was designed to address these limitations by providing integrated dashboards and predictive analytics inside the EMR interface, enabling stakeholders to monitor operational performance and compliance in near real time.

Internal leadership has positioned the platform as a strategic product capability within the TechCare ecosystem. The analytics suite became a prominent differentiator during sales cycles and contract renewals with correctional healthcare agencies.
