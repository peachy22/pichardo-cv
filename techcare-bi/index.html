Leading Development and GTM of a Healthcare Analytics Platform

BACKGROUND

NaphCare, LLC is a healthcare services provider and developer of the electronic medical record platform TechCare used in correctional facilities. Between 2023 and 2024 I led the conceptualization, development, and go-to-market strategy for TechCareBI, an analytics platform built on cloud frameworks and embedded directly within the TechCare ecosystem. The goal was to introduce operational analytics and predictive insights into the daily workflow of clinicians, administrators, and executive leadership.

Correctional healthcare environments involve complex intake processes, medication workflows, regulatory oversight, and strict documentation requirements. Historically, reporting in these environments was fragmented and retrospective. I delivered TechCareBI as a modern data analytics suite to address these limitations, enabling stakeholders to monitor operational performance and compliance in real time. The serving layer is supported by cloud-based ELT pipelines for which I am the lead developer and platform SME.

Key outcomes have not been limited to 100% reductions in manual data compilation efforts. Internal leadership has positioned TechCareBI as a strategic product capability within the TechCare ecosystem: it is recognized as a prominent competitive differentiator during sales cycles and contract renewals. Recently, I led the analytics portion of RFP response that resulted the award with Georgia DOC, our largest EMR vendor contract to date.
